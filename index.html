AboutMe

Hi! I'm Aditi Jha, a passionate UI/UX Designer with a background in Web Development and Computer Engineering. I am deeply fascinated by how design can shape user experiences and make digital interactions intuitive and enjoyable. Whether it's creating sleek user interfaces or diving into the intricate details of design systems, I thrive on bringing ideas to life with a user-centered approach.

Outside of my professional life, I’m an avid reader, always on the lookout for new books that expand my knowledge and creativity. I also love playing badminton – it's a great way for me to stay active and unwind. In addition, I find peace and creativity in drawing mandalas, a hobby that allows me to tap into my artistic side while cultivating patience and mindfulness.

I'm constantly learning and evolving, and I look forward to applying my skills and passion to create meaningful design solutions!
